Thanks for chosing Noted! noted isn't super advanced, but we do have keybinds to make your life just a little easier from everything that may occur in your day to day life.

esc - escape key to unfocus the editor

. - dot key to focus editor.

ctrl s - control + s key to download your file.

control + a + backspace - control + a selects everything, backspace deletes everything.

control + a + c - control + a selects everything, c copies everything.
